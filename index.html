Ingredients

  • 1.75 cups flour
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1.75 cups granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 0.5 cup canola oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1.5 cup milk
9 inch glass pan

Preheat oven to 350 deg F

Mix dry ingredients in one bowl and the wet ingredients in a separate bowl.

Wisk the dry and wet ingredients together and poar into greesed pan.

Bake for 23-26 minutes or until done.
